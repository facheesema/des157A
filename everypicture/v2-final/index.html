pick a disc...dont be shy

*Anastasia*

to this day, this movie's soundcrack still makes me cry and it relives my childhood nostalgia. its my favorite movie. i remember always watching and playing this movie on my dvd player during roadtrips and skipping to the end credits to listen to the song and constantly replay it. the disc comes with a lil feature where you can follow the artists of the movie and draw the main character which is pretty cool.

Barbie Fairytopia

i AM a barbie fanatic and this movie started it all. it's old but gold, but i love it no matter what. you can never beat the OG shitty animation quality

Shrek

this absolute banger gifted us shrek 2. without shrek, there is nothing i fear. it taught me love has no bounds, just curses.

*Oliver and Company*

i ugly cry every time i watch this oh my god i wish i wasnt joking. the lil orange cat reminds me of my own orange cat i now own and it just makes me so happy. honorable moment (my favorite) is when the dogs start singing while disrupting traffic in new york city.

Flushed Away

the part of the movie where the rat gets kicked in the balls back to back in different ways has stayed in my mind since i was 7 years old and im happy to know i can replay it every time. the singing slugs were like oprah to me

Thumbelina

NOT to be confused with the wacky ass barbie thumbelina version OR that other booty thumbelina version thats out there somewhere. this movie was such a fever dream i cant believe i even remember it
